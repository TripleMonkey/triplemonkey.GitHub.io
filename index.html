ACCESSIBLE

Can I play with madness?

As a developer with multiple cognitive impairments, I understand the importance of accessibility for ALL. I earned my Trusted Tester Certification to understand better the accessibility divide that prevents many from realizing the benefits of human invention and ingenuity. This website showcases the care I take to ensure an accessible experience for all. Life is not fair. I'm here to do my part in leveling the playing field.

CREATIVE

Why doesn't mine look like yours?

TripleMonkey Studio is unlike any other. While many strive to be different to fit in, I'm different by design. My creativity is the natural culmination of knowledge, curiosity, and a desire to improve. I enjoy the intrinsic reward of creating a unique solution to common problems before peeking at the answer key for the accepted resolution.

DETAILED

Quality or Quantity?

My attention to detail is often mistaken for perfectionism or exceedingly high expectations. That same attention to detail differentiates professional quality projects from amateur attempts. In my years of experience as an amateur at everything new, I have learned that the only difference in achieving professional results with amateur experience is the time it takes to complete. And the additional time to get it right is always worth it. Do you want a hundred rough widgets today that sometimes work or ten thousand polished widgets tomorrow that always work? The difference is in the details.
