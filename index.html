you are visiting TripleMonkey Studio, home to artist and independent software developer Nigel Krajewski.

why does this place exist

TripleMonkey Studio serves as the portfolio of Nigel Krajewski. included here, you will find a showcase of individuality, graphic and mobile design sense, and the ability to bring creations to life.

who are you

I am Nigel—another starving artist saving the world one day at a time. natural ingenuity drives me to embrace the DIY lifestyle and create new from the old. I proudly support diversity and continually question the status quo. we are all equal and should share our knowledge freely with the world.
